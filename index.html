Best Desserts in Seattle

This is just my opinion, I am biased

  1. Nana's Green Tea
    2. They are known for their green tea but I recommend the black seseme ice cream
  2. Milkie Milkie Dessert Cafe
    3. Korean shaved ice that tastes like cream. A must try!
  3. Hood Famous Bakeshop
    4. Known for their Ube cheesecake. Ube is purple yam.
  4. Central District Ice Cream Company
    5. Ube and coffee ice cream is a must. Also can't go wrong with matcha and mango
